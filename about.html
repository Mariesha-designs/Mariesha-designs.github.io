My Journey

Hello! I’m Mariesha Marshall, an aspiring web designer from Orlando, Florida, with a background in Digital Media and a focus on Web Design and Social Platforms. Currently, I work as an Online Course Accessibility Assistant, where I’m dedicated to ensuring that all students have an inclusive and accessible experience online. I am passionate about creating digital experiences that are intuitive, user-friendly, and accessible for everyone. Whether it’s through internships or personal projects, my design philosophy centers on empowering users of all abilities, making every interaction meaningful and memorable. Outside of design and coding, I find inspiration in painting and playing video games, which not only fuel my creativity but also influence my design process. I am committed to using my skills to craft experiences that truly make a difference.
